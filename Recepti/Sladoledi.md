# Sladoledi

## Cokoloda

- 2 cup milk
- 1.5 cup cream
- 5 egg yoaks
- 1 cup sugar
- pinch of salt
- 8 ounce chocolate

## Limun

- 500 ml cream
- 250 ml milk
- 5 limuna
- 2/3 cups of sugar
- 8 egg yoaks

## Lemon Gelato

- 500 ml (2 cups) whole milk
- 240g (1 cup) sugar
- lemon zest
- 35g mild powder (4 table spoon)
- 5 egg yoaks
- 175g (3/4 cups) lemon juice
- 250ml (1 cup) cream whiping
