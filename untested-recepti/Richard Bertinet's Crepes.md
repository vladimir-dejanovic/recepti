Here’s my Breton family recipe for Crêpes…. The Rum is a must .. just saying! 😂 
@thebertinetkitchen 
Crepes recipe 
500 milk 
250 flour 
100 sugar 
4 whole egg
2 egg yolk 
50 g melted butter 
Table spoon veg oil 
Good drop of rum 
Half a glass water little salt. 
Half a potato for greasing the pan and a little oil 

Mix flour salt sugar. 
Add eggs and oil and butter mix well 
Add milk little by little 
Add water 
Add rum 
If any lumps pass through a sieve 
Leave the batter to rest for at least 15 mi. 

Make crepe
