# Butter Biscuits - Prague

## Ingridients
- 200g butter
- 400-450g flour
- 1 egg
- 100g powdered sugar
- pinch of salt
- cinnamon or cocoa or other spice according to your liking

## Method

All ingridients should be at room temperature. Butter should beslightly hard. Mix the egg with powdered sugar, add butter and a pinch of salt. Mix it well and add sieved flour and cinnamon (or other spice). Knead the mixture well (or mix in a food processor) until combined. Put some flour on your kneading board and roll it well with plain rolling pin. Dust some flour on your engraved rolling pin (this will stop the pastry from sticking to your rolling pin) and put some more flour on your pastry. Now roll your engraved rolling pin¥ over your pastry with both hands. Cut your cookies using cookie cutters and leave them in a cool place. Pre-heat the oven to 200 C and put your cookies on the upper shelf for about 7 minutes or until lightly brown.

## Care Instructions

Before first use, rub the rolling pin with the vegetable oil. After every use, clean it under warm running tap, using brush with a little bit of washing up liquid
